Company Owner

Company owner Keith Looper has been performing HVAC services for over 25 years. He attended Greenville Technical College where he received his associates degree to perform HVAC services. He is a licensed mechanical contractor. To stay current in his profession he attends professional seminars, seeks new certifications, and remains up to date on current literature.

Keith enjoys his business because he has a close bond with his customers. Running a family business allows him to guarantee quality work because he oversees every job personally.

When not working Keith enjoys spending time with his family, hunting, and fishing.
